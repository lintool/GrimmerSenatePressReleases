August 30  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  1 Million For Stewart International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      Federal funds will be used to improve terminal building                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          Washington  DC    Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced the U S  Department of Transportation has awarded  1 105 800 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to Stewart International Airport   The federal dollars come from an Airport Improvement grant   The funds will be used to improve the terminal building        This is great news for the Hudson Valley   said Schumer   Stewart International Airport welcomes travelers to New York each year  and these improvements are important for the airport  as well as strengthening the local economy  I will continue to fight tooth and nail for federal funds to help the airport remain a thriving center of activity and a hub for economic growth in the region         This funding is a very welcome investment in Stewart International Airport and in the entire Hudson Valley region     Senator Clinton said    For tourism and business alike  access is an essential ingredient for economic development  I am pleased that we can continue to support the airport with this federal funding because more flights mean better economic opportunities for communities         These federal funds will be used by Stewart International Airport to improve the terminal building   The project will enhance passenger services and ensure that the facilities will meet the needs of the growing community      Last week  Schumer and Clinton announced  250 000 in federal funds was awarded to Stewart Airport by the U S  Department of Transportation   The federal dollars come from the Small Community Air Service Development Program  SCASDP  Grants   The funds will be used to help the airport promote its services to passengers and offer inducements to new carriers  Stewart Airport will use these federal funds to institute a comprehensive marketing program to recruit low far service to Florida
