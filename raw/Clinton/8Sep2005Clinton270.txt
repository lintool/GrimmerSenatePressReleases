September 8  2005 Schumer  Clinton Announce Over  16 5 Million for Greater Rochester International Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         Washington  DC    Senators Charles E  Schumer and Hillary Rodham Clinton today announced the U S  Department of Transportation has awarded   16 926 112 in Federal Aviation Administration  FAA  funds to Greater Rochester International Airport   The federal dollars come from an Airport Improvement grant and will be used for runway safety and improvement at the airport               Rochester airport s passenger growth is a good sign for the local economy and these improvements will only make the airport safer  easier to use  and an engine for more job creation   Schumer said   Coming on the heels of our federal funds for Renaissance Square  this announcement couldn t have come at a better time              This funding is great news for Greater Rochester International Airport   Senator Clinton said   For tourism and business alike  access and safety at the airport are essential for efficient operation  I am pleased that we can continue to support the airport with this federal funding because more flights mean better economic opportunities for communities             These federal grants fall under the Airport Improvement Program  AIP   The project will enhance passenger services and ensure that the facilities meet the needs of the growing community
